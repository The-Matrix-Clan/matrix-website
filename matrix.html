ABOUT US

Founded in 2000, the Matrix Clan at Mount St. Mary School, Delhi Cantt., has evolved into a standout technology club in Delhi. Primarily composed of students from classes XI and XII, it also actively involves all Marians in exploring new technological frontiers. The club's strong presence at inter-school events underscores its reputation for technological prowess and innovation, consistently making an impact across the city.

Central to its activities is Matrix Ecomm Psynapse, an annual event co-founded by the club, which has become a cornerstone of the school's technological calendar. This event not only showcases the talents and innovations of students but also serves as a platform for them to demonstrate their skills and creativity. Beyond organizing events, the Matrix Clan plays a pivotal role in supporting various technological and design initiatives within the school, contributing significantly to its technological environment.

At its core, the Matrix Clan embodies a motto that reflects its mission: "to make a dent in the universe, one keystroke at a time." This guiding principle motivates members to excel in innovation and creativity, driving them to contribute meaningfully to both everyday projects and major school-wide endeavors. Their dedication to this ethos permeates every aspect of their activities, solidifying their role as leaders in the school's technological and design landscape.

MATRIX ECOMM PSYNAPSE

The wait is over. The 25th Bro. Aloysius Matrix Ecomm Psynapse fest is back and better than ever! This isn't just any event; it's the most exciting one of the year, showing off skills and smart ideas in many areas. The fest is all about getting people interested in Computer Science, Economics, and Psychology, and finding the best young talents from around the world. Participants will face tough challenges in technology, business, economics, and psychology.

What makes this fest special is that there’s no help given; you have to rely on your own skills and determination. Only the best will succeed, making it a true test of talent and expertise. The stakes are high, and it's a place where only the exceptional thrive.

This year's fest will be an unforgettable experience with chances for learning, meeting new people, and showing off your talents. It’s not just an event; it’s a journey of growth and excellence. There’s no reason to miss it. Get ready to challenge yourself and rise to the occasion because in this arena, every moment counts. After all, this is bat country.
